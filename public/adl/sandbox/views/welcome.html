What is the Sandbox?
The Sandbox is a multi-user, collaborative, open source virtual environment that lives in the cloud. We allow uses to create 'worlds' where they can interact, play, and build. Our system comes with a set of authoring tools that allow you to build games or simulations and share them with the world.
Worlds
Browse Worlds. The Sandbox allows you to create your very own world. Our servers will save and track your world, allowing you to access it from anywhere. Worlds can be games, simulations, even scientific visualizations. Or, you could just build a place to hang out. You can use our in-browser editing tools to build or import 3D models and add behaviors or scripts. The Sandbox authoring environment includes a wide vareity of features to help you be creative. Worlds are even multi-player, so you can join other users or invite them to collaborate with you.
Examples
Browse all samples. These sample environments demonstrate many features of the platform, and serve as test cases as well. Most sample environments are 'Published', meaning that the database will not track changes to the worlds. Many are 'Single Player', so you won't be running into anyone else while you check them out. Also, since they are single player, you can interact without logging in.
Tutorial
